Detailed Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Exposed! We're committed to protecting your privacy while providing an entertaining party game experience. This policy explains our data practices in clear, honest terms.

2. Who We Are

Exposed is developed and operated by AFLA Games. You can contact us at:

  • Email: aflagames@outlook.com
  • Developer: AFLA Games
  • App Category: Entertainment/Games

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Data Stored Locally (on your device only):

  • Player names you enter
  • Game scores and statistics
  • Game preferences
  • Previous game history

3.2 System Data:

  • Basic crash reports through Apple's standard system
  • Anonymous usage statistics via App Store
  • Device performance data for game optimization

3.3 What We DON'T Collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or media
  • Microphone data
  • Messages or communications
  • Browser history
  • Device ID or tracking information

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Game Functionality:

  • Store player names for convenience in future games
  • Track game progress and scores
  • Maintain game settings and preferences

4.2 App Improvement:

  • Identify and fix crashes
  • Optimize game performance
  • Improve user experience

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 We Do Not:

  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use data for advertising
  • Track users across apps or websites
  • Store data on external servers

5.2 Exceptions:

  • If required by law
  • To protect legal rights
  • With your explicit consent

6. Social Sharing Feature

When you use the "Share Results" feature:

  • Sharing uses your device's native sharing options
  • We don't track or store shared content
  • We don't access your social media accounts

7. Data Security
